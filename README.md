This is a quiz app I made to host a quiz competion.

Participants press the buzzer in the website to register their response and the time of their clicking is recorded and sent to the database. This way we overcome any network latency issues.

There is also a leader board for showcasing who pressed the button when.

Here is the buzzer: https://quiz-3epe.onrender.com/buzzer/
And the leaderboard: https://quiz-3epe.onrender.com/leaderboard/
